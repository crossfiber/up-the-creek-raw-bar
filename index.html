A Local Icon on the River

Up the Creek Raw Bar isn't just another seafood joint. Since 2013, we've built something authentic: a gathering place where locals and visitors alike come for the freshest oysters, expertly crafted cocktails, and unbeatable river views.

Our oysters are privately farmed from the pristine waters around Alligator Point and Panacea, giving them a unique flavor profile you won't find anywhere else on the Gulf Coast. We hand-select every half shell, every preparation, every pairing.

The vibe is intentionally casual. Order at the counter, grab a beer from one of our 12 rotating craft taps, find a spot on the deck overlooking the Apalachicola River, and settle in. This is how waterfront dining should feel: warm, unpretentious, lived-in, and real.

Frequently Asked Questions

We operate on a casual, counter-service basis where you order at the counter and we bring your food to your table. While we don't take formal reservations, we have plenty of covered and open-air deck space. For large groups, we recommend calling ahead at 850-653-2525 so we can make accommodations.
Yes, we have convenient parking available for our guests. We're located at 313 Water Street in downtown Apalachicola. Street parking and nearby lots are easily accessible. If you need specific parking information for a large group, please call us at 850-653-2525.
Absolutely! We love dogs. Your furry friends are welcome on our outdoor deck areas. It's one of the best places in Apalachicola to enjoy a meal with your dog while overlooking the river.
Our oysters are privately farmed from the pristine waters around Alligator Point and Panacea, Florida. This gives them a unique flavor profile that reflects the Gulf Coast terroir. We hand-select every oyster and control the farming process to ensure the highest quality.
We have several naturally gluten-free options available, including all of our raw oysters, steamed oysters, grilled fish and shrimp, and many salads. Please speak with our staff about your specific dietary needs. For safety and accuracy, please call us at 850-653-2525 to discuss your requirements.
Yes, we offer takeout! You can order at the counter and take your meal with you. Many items travel well, and we package everything carefully. Call ahead at 850-653-2525 if you have a large order.
Yes, we are wheelchair accessible and have elevator access. Our facilities are designed to accommodate all guests comfortably. If you have specific accessibility questions, please call us at 850-653-2525.
We're open Monday through Saturday, noon to 8pm. We're closed Sundays. These hours can occasionally change during off-season, so please call 850-653-2525 to confirm before your visit, especially during winter months.
