About Up the Creek

Sitting right on the Apalachicola River, Up the Creek Raw Bar has been serving fresh oysters and Gulf seafood since 2013. We're all about simple, quality ingredients and a genuine welcome.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick oyster and a beer, or settling in for an afternoon of shrimp baskets and river views, we've got you covered. Our oysters come from local waters, and everything else is sourced with care.

Come see us at 313 Water Street, right on the water. We're open most days, and the river is always beautiful.

Questions?

What are your hours?

We're open Monday through Saturday, Noon to 8pm. Closed Sunday. Call ahead for holiday hours at 850-653-2525.

Do you take reservations?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis, but you can call ahead to get an idea of wait times. For larger groups, we recommend calling 850-653-2525.

Where are you located?

We're at 313 Water Street in Apalachicola, Florida, right on the Apalachicola River. Easy parking and a beautiful waterfront view.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes. We have salads, sides, and other options. Call 850-653-2525 or check our menu for details.

Can I buy oysters to go?

Yes. Call 850-653-2525 to place a to-go order for oysters, seafood baskets, or anything else on our menu.
